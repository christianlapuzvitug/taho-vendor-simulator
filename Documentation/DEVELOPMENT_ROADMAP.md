# Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: Pre-Production
- Define game concept and mechanics
- Create initial art and narrative design
- Assemble team and assign roles
- Develop project plan and timelines

## Phase 2: Production
- Build core gameplay mechanics
- Implement art assets and sound
- Conduct regular playtesting and iterations
- Establish marketing plan and community engagement strategies

## Phase 3: Alpha Testing
- Release alpha version to selected testers
- Gather feedback and identify major issues
- Continue refining gameplay and fixing bugs

## Phase 4: Beta Testing
- Open beta to a wider audience
- Focus on stability, balance, and polish
- Finalize marketing materials and Steam page

## Phase 5: Launch
- Prepare for Steam submission and launch
- Execute marketing plan on launch day
- Monitor user feedback and address post-launch issues
