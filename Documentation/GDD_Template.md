# Game Design Document (GDD) Template

## 1. Game Overview
### 1.1 Game Title
- Enter the title of the game.

### 1.2 Genre
- Specify the genre (e.g., platformer, adventure, RPG).

### 1.3 Platform
- Define the platforms (PC, console, mobile).

### 1.4 Target Audience
- Detail the target demographic.

## 2. Gameplay
### 2.1 Core Mechanics
- Describe the main mechanics of the game.

### 2.2 Game Rules
- Outline the rules governing the gameplay.

### 2.3 Progression
- Detail how players progress through the game.

## 3. Story
### 3.1 Plot Summary
- Provide a brief summary of the game's story.

### 3.2 Characters
- Outline the main characters and their roles.

## 4. Art and Design
### 4.1 Visual Style
- Describe the art style (e.g., 2D, 3D, pixel art).

### 4.2 User Interface
- Outline the design and layout of the UI.

## 5. Sound and Music
### 5.1 Sound Effects
- Detail the required sound effects.

### 5.2 Music
- Describe the musical score and themes.

## 6. Technical Specifications
### 6.1 Engine
- Specify the game engine used (e.g., Unity, Unreal).

### 6.2 Performance Targets
- Outline the performance goals (frame rate, resolution).

## 7. Development Timeline
### 7.1 Milestones
- List major milestones and deadlines.

### 7.2 Team Roles
- Define the roles and responsibilities of the team members.

## 8. Market Analysis
### 8.1 Competitor Analysis
- Analyze competitors in the market.

### 8.2 Marketing Strategy
- Outline the strategy for promoting the game.

## 9. Appendix
### 9.1 References
- Include any references or additional notes.
